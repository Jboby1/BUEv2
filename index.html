Welcome to the BUEv2 (Biggest Update Ever Version 2), the unofficial second version of TF2Tightrope's "Biggest Update Ever"!

This is a fan-made fake update for Team Fortress 2 which hosts many original ideas for weapons, maps, cosmetics, and more from ALL OVER THE INTERNET.

We have discussion threads on Facepunch, TF2Maps, and the Steam Group, so hop on over there to leave your suggestions!


The custom models and maps featured on this site are made by talented people who are not affiliated with the BUEv2.

To support their hard work, click on the images of their content.
