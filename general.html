General Changes

Added several promotional cosmetics and weapons (such as the Bolt-Action Blitzer, Black Rose, Iron Curtain, Big Kill, ect.) to the drop pool, as the games they could be achieved from have shut down.

Copies of these items obtained before this update are now Genuine quality, and tradable


The Grordbort pack laser weapons now deal fire damage instead of their original respective damage types

This change does not allow them to deal afterburn


Revamped inventory system with new options including multi-select, mass deletion, search, and additional/smarter auto-sort options


Added "New Zealium" weapons, which can be obtained from a brand-new Chemistry Set by inputting 200 Refined Metal and a class or slot token

Strangeifiers for these weapons can be crafted at any time, by inputting 100 Refined Metal and either a Civilian Grade Stat Clock or a pre-existing Strange weapon


Reintroduced Collector's-quality Chemistry Sets, and expanded the pool of collectable items to include all new and pre-existing weapons and cosmetics

A very rare "Mechanic-Type" Chemistry Set has also been added, which accepts tools, paints, crates and keys-- outputs are unusable, but come with a special description!

Crafting items (metal, tokens, ect.) used in "Mechanic-Type" Chemistry Sets retain their craftability, and can be used to craft more Collector's-quality items!


Added a new item to the Mann Co. Store-- "The Mann Co. Resale Lock-Picking Kit"

Can be used on expired or discontinued Mann Co. Supply Crates to allow them to be opened with a standard Mann Co. Supply Crate Key!


Fixed being able to negate a slowdown penalty by strafing


Multi-class Weapon Rebalances

Restored the B.A.S.E. Jumper's air control and "updraft" ability

Restored the Reserve Shooter's ability mini-crit airblasted enemies
